What is Bumi?

Where farmers thrive and communities grow.

Bumi is Malaysia's first farm-to-community super app — part marketplace, part hiring platform, part social community. Think LinkedIn meets Grab, meets a neighbourhood kebun. Rooted in the ethics of permaculture: Earth Care, People Care, Fair Share.

List and sell fresh produce, seeds, seedlings, and agri-products
Offer and book farm consultations, workshops, and farm visits
Join community garden plots and co-invest in shared growing land
Everything is free — pay only if you want organic or halal verification

Three pillars

Everything a growing community needs.

Marketplace — Kedai Bumi
Browse and buy fresh produce, seeds, seedlings, and compost direct from local farmers. Delivery or self-pickup. Verified sellers. Free to list.
Hire a Farmer — Cari Petani
Book a certified farmer like booking a Grab. Permaculture design, farm consultations, workshops, farm visits. Rated and reviewed professionals.
Community — Komuniti Bumi
A living feed of harvests, knowledge, and shared growing. Join community plots, co-invest in shared land, share your journey. Build food sovereignty together.
Permaculture principles in the platform

Not just a marketplace. A way of living.

01
Earth Care
Every listing defaults to regenerative. Verified organic practices are recognised and visible on every farm profile.
02
People Care
Farmers pay nothing to list. The platform earns only through optional verification — never by taking from those who can least afford it.
03
Fair Share
Surplus produce finds community buyers. Community plots distribute harvests equitably. Knowledge shared openly. No paywalls on learning.
04
Observe & Interact
Farmers log growing conditions, soil health, and harvests. The community feed is a living record of what works — season by season, zone by zone.
How it works

From soil to screen in four steps.

1
Create your profile
Free for every farmer. List your land, methods, produce, and services. No subscription. No commission.
2
Discover & connect
Browse by location, produce type, or farming method. Follow farmers. Join plots. Book services.
3
Transact safely
Secure in-app payments. Delivery or pickup. Escrow held until delivery confirmed. Reviews both ways.
4
Grow together
Share harvests on the community feed. Log your plot. Learn from peers. Build food sovereignty.
