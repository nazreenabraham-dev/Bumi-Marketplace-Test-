What is Bumi?

Where farmers thrive and communities grow.

Bumi is Malaysia's first farm-to-community super app — part marketplace, part hiring platform, part social community. Rooted in permaculture ethics: Earth Care, People Care, Fair Share. Every feature is free. We earn only when you choose to prove your standards.

List and sell produce, seeds, seedlings, agri-products
Offer farm consultations, workshops, and farm visits
Join community garden plots and co-invest in shared land
100% free — pay only for optional organic or halal verification
Permaculture in the platform

Not just a marketplace. A way of living.

01
Earth Care
Every listing defaults to regenerative. Verified organic practices visible on every farm profile.
02
People Care
Farmers pay nothing to list. We earn only through optional verification — never from those who can least afford it.
03
Fair Share
Surplus produce finds community buyers. Harvests shared equitably. Knowledge open, never paywalled.
04
Observe & Interact
Farmers log soil health and harvests. The community feed is a living record of what works — season by season.

How verification works

Honest, transparent, earned.

01
Apply online
Submit your farm details, growing methods, soil practices, and evidence of your approach.
02
Physical audit
A trained Bumi verifier visits your farm, documents practices, and assesses compliance.
03
Badge awarded
Your verified status appears on every listing, your profile, and in all search results on Bumi.
04
Annual renewal
A lightweight annual check ensures your practices remain consistent. Renewal is RM 49 or RM 99.
