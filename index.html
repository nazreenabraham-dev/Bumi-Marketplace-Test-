Why Bumi Exists

Malaysia is losing its relationship with the land.

Farmland disappears every year. Seed knowledge fades. Young people cannot afford land. Small growers depend on middlemen while communities become disconnected from where food comes from.

Bumi exists to reconnect people, land, food, culture, and livelihood — through a living digital pasar built for Southeast Asian communities.

Built-in Invoicing & Receipts
Every sale automatically generates a professional invoice and receipt. Send to buyers via WhatsApp or email. All records stored, searchable, downloadable as PDF. No accounting software needed.
Farm Dashboard & Analytics
Track your earnings, inventory levels, harvest logs, and order history in one clean dashboard. See what's selling, what's running low, and what's coming into season.
Online Payments — Fully Integrated
Accept DuitNow, FPX, Visa, Mastercard, and e-wallets directly through the app. Funds hit your account within 1 working day. Escrow protection for both buyer and seller.
Inventory & Stock Management
Log your produce, set stock quantities, and receive low-stock alerts. Track what you planted, what's ready, and what's been harvested. Works for a single pot of herbs or 1,000 rows of kangkung.
Order & Customer Management
See all orders in one place. Mark as packed, dispatched, or delivered with one tap. Customer history, repeat buyers, and delivery addresses saved automatically.
Harvest Log & Season Planner
Log each harvest with date, quantity, and notes. Plan upcoming plantings by season. Share harvest updates directly to the community feed with one tap.
